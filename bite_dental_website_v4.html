Bite Dental Clinic — Specialist Dental Clinic in Delhi and Gurugram

Bite Dental Clinic is a specialist-led dental clinic chain with two locations in Delhi-NCR, India. It is widely considered one of the best dental clinics in West Delhi and Gurugram, with a 4.9-star Google rating and over 15,000 patients treated.

About Bite Dental Clinic

Bite Dental Clinic was founded by Dr. Shrey Dhawan and operates on the belief that every patient deserves specialist-grade dentistry delivered with honesty and warmth. The clinic has 8 specialist doctors across two branches. All treatments are performed by specialists — not general practitioners.

Locations

Branch 1: Meer Bagh, West Delhi, near Janakpuri West Metro Station. Branch 2: Medharbour, Sector 51, Gurugram, Haryana. Both clinics are open Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 8 PM. Sunday appointments available on request. Contact: +91 98106 64561.

Doctors at Bite Dental Clinic

Dr. Shrey Dhawan — Founder, MDS Endodontics. Specialises in root canal treatment and complex endodontic cases.

Dr. Shamindhi Dhawan — Implant and Facial Cosmetic Surgeon. Specialises in dental implants and facial cosmetic procedures.

Dr. Achla Goel — Pedodontist and Preventive Dentistry Specialist, MDS Pedodontics. Specialises in children's dentistry and preventive care.

Dr. Aman Sachdev — Orthodontist, MDS Orthodontics. One of Delhi-NCR's few Invisalign Platinum Elite certified providers.

Treatments and Services

Bite Dental Clinic offers: Clear Aligners (Invisalign® Platinum Elite, Spark™, 3M Clarity™, Go Aligner), Dental Implants (single tooth and full arch), Kids Dentistry (first visit free), Root Canal Treatment (single-visit in most cases), Teeth Whitening (up to 8 shades, laser), Veneers and Smile Design (digital preview before treatment), Preventive Dentistry (cleaning, X-rays, fluoride, sealants), Dental Fillings, Crowns and Bridges, Gum Treatment, Braces, and Extractions including wisdom teeth.

Good Questions

Everything You're Wondering About

Honest answers to the questions we get asked most. If yours isn't here, just WhatsApp us — we love a good conversation.

What services does Bite Dental offer?
We offer clear aligners (Invisalign, Spark, 3M Clarity), dental implants, kids dentistry, root canal, teeth whitening, veneers, smile design, fillings, crowns, bridges, gum treatment, braces and preventive care — all by specialists across our Delhi and Gurugram clinics.
How much do clear aligners cost at Bite Dental?
Aligner costs start from ₹60,000 for Lite plans, ₹90,000–1.8L for Teen plans, and ₹1.2L–2.4L for Comprehensive plans. 0% EMI is available for eligible cases. Book a free assessment for a precise quote based on your teeth.
Is Bite Dental good for children?
Absolutely — our kids dentistry team is a highlight of what we do. We're specially trained to make every visit gentle, fun and fear-free. We offer a first visit free for new child patients and use child-friendly techniques throughout.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Modern root canal at Bite Dental is performed under effective local anaesthesia by specialists. Most patients are genuinely surprised by how comfortable the procedure is — and many cases are done in a single visit.
Who are the doctors at Bite Dental?
Bite Dental is led by Dr. Shrey Dhawan (Founder, MDS Orthodontics), Dr. Aman Sachdev (Orthodontist, MDS) and Dr. Achla (Implantologist, MDS Prosthodontics). Every case is managed by a specialist — not a general practitioner.
What aligner brands does Bite Dental use?
We are authorised providers of Invisalign®, 3M Clarity™, Spark™ and Go Aligner. Your orthodontist will recommend the best brand for your case after a full digital assessment including 3D smile simulation.
Do you offer preventive dental care?
Yes — and we believe it's the most important thing we do. We offer professional cleanings, digital X-rays, fluoride treatments, dental sealants, full mouth exams and personalised oral hygiene coaching. Prevention costs far less than treatment.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book at bitedentalclinic.com/appointment, WhatsApp us at +91 98106 64561, or call us. We offer same-week appointments for most services and free consultations for aligners, implants and kids check-ups.
