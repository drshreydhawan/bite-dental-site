Common Questions

Everything You Want to Know

Honest, clear answers — because making a good decision about your dental care starts with having the right information.

What dental services does Bite Dental Clinic offer?
Bite Dental offers clear aligners (Invisalign®, Spark™, 3M Clarity™), dental implants, kids dentistry, root canal treatment, teeth whitening, veneers, and full smile design — all managed by specialists across our Delhi and Gurugram clinics.
How much do clear aligners cost at Bite Dental?
Aligner costs start from approximately ₹60,000 for Lite plans, ₹90,000–1.8L for Teen plans, and ₹1.2L–2.4L for Comprehensive plans. 0% EMI is available on eligible cases. Book a free assessment for a precise quote tailored to your teeth.
Do you offer a 3D smile preview before starting aligners?
Yes — every aligner consultation at Bite Dental includes a 3D digital simulation showing your projected smile result before you commit to treatment. You see the outcome first, then decide.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Modern root canal treatment at Bite Dental is performed under effective local anesthesia by specialists. The procedure itself is virtually painless for most patients. Many cases can be completed in a single visit.
Where are Bite Dental's clinic locations?
Bite Dental has two locations: West Delhi (Meer Bagh, near Janakpuri) and Gurugram (Med Harbour, Sector 31). Both clinics maintain the same specialist team, equipment standards, and protocols.
How long does aligner treatment take?
Mild to moderate cases often complete in 6–12 months. Complex bite corrections may take longer. During your free consultation, our orthodontist will review your case and give you an accurate timeline specific to your treatment.
Is Bite Dental good for children?
Absolutely. Our kids dentistry service is designed specifically for young patients — from first check-ups to habit correction. We use gentle techniques and a child-friendly environment to make dental visits positive experiences from the start.
How do I book an appointment at Bite Dental?
You can book directly at bitedentalclinic.com/appointment, WhatsApp us at +91 98106 64561, or call us. We offer same-week appointments for most services. Free consultations available for aligners and implants.
