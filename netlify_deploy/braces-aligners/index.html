All Orthodontic Options

Pick the treatment that fits your life.

Every option works. The right one depends on your case complexity, lifestyle, and budget. Dr. Aman will walk you through it.

🔩
Metal Braces
The original. Still unbeatable.
Metal braces are the most precise and versatile orthodontic tool ever made. They work for every case — mild to severe — and they never stop working, even if you forget to "comply."
  • Best for complex crowding, spacing and bite correction
  • No compliance needed — they work 24/7
  • Fastest treatment timeline for most cases
  • Most affordable orthodontic option
From ₹35,000 · Full arch · Both jaws
🪩
Ceramic Braces
Discreet. Effective. Low profile.
Ceramic brackets blend with your teeth — from a normal talking distance, they're nearly invisible. Same clinical precision as metal braces, with a much lower visual profile.
  • Tooth-coloured brackets — nearly invisible
  • Great option for adults and working professionals
  • Same correction capability as metal braces
  • Slightly more delicate — dietary care required
From ₹50,000 · Full arch · Both jaws
😁
Clear Aligners
Invisible. Removable. Comfortable.
Custom-printed trays that move your teeth in precise increments — and nobody has to know. Invisalign, Spark, and 3M Clarity are all available at Bite Dental, prescribed and monitored by Dr. Aman.
  • Virtually invisible — nobody knows you're in treatment
  • Remove to eat, brush and floss normally
  • Comfortable — no metal wires or brackets
  • Best for mild to moderate crowding and spacing
From ₹80,000 · Invisalign® / Spark™ / 3M Clarity™
🤫
Lingual Braces
Hidden behind your teeth. Zero visibility.
Lingual braces are bonded to the inside (tongue-side) of your teeth — completely invisible from the front. Same precision as traditional braces, with absolute discretion for professionals who can't wear visible hardware.
  • 100% hidden — no visible braces whatsoever
  • Works for complex cases aligners can't handle
  • Custom-fabricated for your specific tooth anatomy
  • Slightly longer adjustment period for speech
On consultation · Custom fabricated

Initial soreness for 2–3 days after placement (or after each wire change / new aligner tray) is normal — this is your teeth responding to movement. Most patients describe it as pressure, not pain.
