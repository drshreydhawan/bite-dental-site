The Process

From missing tooth to permanent smile.

A well-planned implant procedure is straightforward, predictable and largely painless.

1
Free Consultation & 3D Imaging
Dr. Shamindhi Dhawan examines your case clinically. Digital X-rays and 3D CBCT scans (where needed) assess bone volume and anatomy with precision. You'll receive a clear treatment plan with full transparent pricing before any commitment.
2
Implant Placement Surgery
The titanium implant is placed under local anaesthesia — most patients feel pressure but no pain. The procedure typically takes 45–90 minutes per implant. Most patients return to normal activities the next day.
3
Osseointegration (Healing Phase)
Over the next 3–6 months, the implant fuses permanently with your jawbone — a process called osseointegration. A temporary restoration is provided during this period so you're never without a tooth.
4
Final Crown / Restoration
Once the implant is fully integrated, your custom-fabricated ceramic crown is fitted and adjusted for perfect bite alignment. The result: a natural-looking, fully functional tooth that's built to last a lifetime.
