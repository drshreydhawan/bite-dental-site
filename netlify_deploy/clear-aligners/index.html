The Process

From consultation to confident smile.

Simple, transparent, entirely on your timeline.

1
Free Consultation & 3D Simulation
Dr. Aman Sachdev examines your teeth and bite. Using cutting-edge ClinCheck software, you'll see a 3D animation of your expected result — before any commitment. This is your smile preview, completely free.
2
Custom Aligner Fabrication
Once you approve your simulation, your aligners are precision-fabricated using digital scans of your teeth. No messy impressions. Each set is custom-built for your exact anatomy. Invisalign® aligners are manufactured in the USA to the highest clinical standards.
3
Wear & Progress Reviews
Wear each aligner set for 20–22 hours per day. You'll switch to the next set every 1–2 weeks. Progress check-ups every 6–8 weeks ensure your treatment is tracking exactly as planned. Most patients notice visible changes within the first month.
4
Refinements & Retainers
Once your main treatment is complete, any fine-tuning refinements are included. You'll then move to retainers — worn nightly — to lock in your result permanently. Your new smile is yours to keep.
