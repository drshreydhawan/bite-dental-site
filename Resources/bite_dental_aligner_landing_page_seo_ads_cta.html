FAQs

How long does aligner treatment take?

Mild–moderate cases often complete in 6–12 months. Complex bites may take longer depending on biology and goals.

Is the treatment painful?

Expect gentle pressure for 24–48 hours after switching trays. We share comfort tips and pacing to minimise soreness.

How many hours must I wear aligners?

20–22 hours daily. Remove only for eating, drinking (except water), and brushing.

Can I eat and drink with aligners?

Remove aligners to eat or drink anything except water. Brush/rinse before reinserting.

How do I clean my aligners?

Rinse with cool water and a soft brush. Avoid hot water and coloured mouthwashes that can stain or warp plastic.

What are attachments and IPR?

Tooth‑coloured bumps (attachments) and minimal enamel polishing (IPR) help precise movements and create space when indicated.

What if I lose or crack an aligner?

Contact us immediately. Depending on progress, we may advise moving to the next tray, reverting to the previous, or ordering a replacement.

Will aligners affect my speech?

A brief lisp (1–3 days) is common as you adapt. Reading aloud speeds it up.

Do you treat open bite, deep bite, or crossbite?

Yes. Many bite corrections are possible with doctor‑led planning. Severe skeletal cases may need braces or adjuncts first.

Can I whiten during treatment?

Yes, typically between stages or with supervised protocols. We’ll advise timing to avoid sensitivity.

What about gum disease or cavities?

We stabilise gums and treat decay first; a healthy foundation improves outcomes.

How often are reviews?

Usually every 6–8 weeks, in‑clinic or remote, adjusted to your case.

What if I need refinements?

Small plan adjustments are common. Comprehensive plans include refinements; Lite plans have add‑on options.

Are aligners safe during pregnancy?

Clear aligners are non‑invasive; we avoid X‑rays unless essential. Please share your OB clearance if available.

How much do aligners cost? Do you offer EMI?

Typical ranges: Lite ₹ ~60k–1.1L, Comprehensive ₹ ~1.2L–2.4L, Teen ₹ ~90k–1.8L. 0% EMI available for eligible cases.

What retainers do you provide?

Short‑term retainers in comprehensive plans; long‑term options (Vivera™/Essix/bonded) available.

Do I have to wear retainers forever?

Teeth naturally drift. Nightly wear keeps results stable; we’ll personalise your retainer plan.

Can I switch from braces to aligners?

Often yes, depending on case progress and goals. We’ll assess and advise.

Can teens use aligners?

Yes—Teen plans include wear indicators and eruption guides; parent monitoring available.

Do coffee or smoking stain aligners?

Yes—remove trays for coffee/tea; avoid smoking with aligners in to prevent staining and odour.

Travelling with aligners?

Carry your next set and a travel case; keep a spare retainer and a copy of your plan if possible.
